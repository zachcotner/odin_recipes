Hotdog

Description

  • Hotdogs (1 pack)
  • Hotdog Buns (1 pack)
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese
  • Diced Onion
  • Relish

Directions

  1. Grill hotdogs
  2. Warm buns
  3. Place hotdogs into warmed buns
  4. Top with preferred condiments
  5. Serve with preferred sides (chips, potato salad, baked beans, etc)
